I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Information Systems, Singapore Management University (SMU). I am very fortunate to work under the supervision of Professor David Lo. I received B.Eng. and M.S. in Computer Science from the Ho Chi Minh University of Technology - Vietnam (2009) and Konkuk University - South Korea (2011), respectively. From 2012 to 2016, I worked as a research engineer at Ghent University (2012-2015) and the Living Analytics Research Centre, SMU (2015-2016).

My research interests include software engineering and machine learning, with particular focus on software bug localization, defect prediction, and program representation.

People usually call me James.
